## 📋 Step-by-Step Deployment to Streamlit Cloud

### **Step 1: Go to Streamlit Cloud**
1. Open: https://streamlit.io/cloud
2. Click **"Start for free"** or **"Sign in"**

### **Step 2: Connect GitHub Account**
1. Click **"Continue with GitHub"**
2. Authorize Streamlit to access your repositories
3. Choose repository: **field-activity-dashboard**

### **Step 3: Deploy the App**
1. Click **"Deploy an app"** button
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Repository**: eesh08/field-activity-dashboard
   - **Branch**: main
   - **Main file path**: app.py
3. Click **"Deploy!"**

### **Step 4: Wait for Deployment** ⏳
- Streamlit builds and deploys your app
- Takes about 2-3 minutes
- You'll see: "✅ Your app is now live!"

### **Step 5: Get Your Public URL**
- Your dashboard will be at: `https://field-activity-dashboard-<random>.streamlit.app`
- Share this URL with your team!
